What Am I?

In Greek mythology, I am an eagle, the symbol of Zeus, the most powerful of the gods. In Celtic culture, I am the symbol of rebirth and renewal. For the ancient Egyptians, I am the symbol of eternal life.

We are many species of eagles, we stand out for their large size, with a length of up to one meter, vast plumage, curved beak and large eyes. We have an extraordinary visual capacity, eight times more accurate than human vision. We see prey from very high and usually feed on small mammals, reptiles or fish.
